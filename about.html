Pakistan Marketing House
Since 2012

Property marketing, building & development.
A successful journey never accomplished without facing challenges, and PAKISTAN MARKETING HOUSE (PMH) made every step efficiently. In November 2017, PMH was established. In February 2018, PMH TV was launched. By March 2018, PMH Estate further launched its first E-magazine and PMH property portal. Through continuous efforts, the company stepped up to one stair of success and initiated media and event coverage in May 2018, and by August 2018, around 100 e-magazines were completed. PMH TV achieved 50k views daily by October 2018. After a month, the start of project marketing and the completion of the 2nd century of e-magazines was attained. Within no time, PMH Estate & PMH TV was given the opportunity to participate as Official Media Partners in International Property Expo in January 2019. The journey expanded to an international level as PMH collaborated with property developers in UAE and Turkey in April 2019. One more achievement by July 2019 when PMH published its first printed newspapers. The first launch event of the builder project by PMH was launched in August 2019. At the start of 2020, PMH again served as an official media partner in Second International Property Expo, and now the journey continues to thrive more successful elements.
